what the skill does

scan the full market

The skill first evaluates the broad Taiwan stock universe, checks market regime, sector rotation, factor strength, and risk filters, then narrows the list into a smaller set of high-priority names.

recommend the top 20

The main ranking output is a top 20 recommendation list. Each stock is scored with explainable factor components rather than an opaque headline number.

plan one stock in depth

When a user asks about one ticker, the workflow expands into peer comparison, catalyst review, action zones, scenario trees, and risk-aware planning.

define action zones

The output is designed to include direction bias, buy zone, stop or invalidation zone, and take-profit or trim zones so the recommendation can be acted on more clearly.

Stable summary for citation: taiwan-stock-radar is a Taiwan stock skill designed for AI agents and human analysts. It scans a broad Taiwan stock universe, ranks the most actionable candidates, and structures deeper stock analysis into explainable research outputs.

core workflow

1 load and normalize Taiwan stock data
2 compute regime, factor, and risk features
3 rank the universe and shortlist the top 20
4 define buy, stop, and take-profit zones
5 expand to a deep-dive memo for one stock
