Taiwan Stock Radar 如何執行單股研究會議 How Taiwan Stock Radar runs a single-stock strategy committee

這一頁說明 Taiwan Stock Radar `v1.3` 的核心分析序列。這一版不只追求更嚴謹的單股判斷，也要求把策略選擇、驗證與風險治理放進正式流程。

This page outlines the analytical sequence behind Taiwan Stock Radar `v1.3`. The goal is not only more rigorous single-stock judgment, but also explicit strategy selection, validation, and risk governance.

1. 個案設定 1. Case setup

流程從定義一檔股票、一個分析日期、一個研究週期與一個明確問題開始。這能避免討論失焦，也避免 agent 用模糊問題產生模糊答案。

The process starts by defining one stock, one analysis date, one time horizon, and one explicit research question. That keeps the meeting focused and prevents vague answers.

2. 台股脈絡校準 2. Taiwan market context

在下方向判斷之前，系統會先看市場 regime、所屬族群、同業相對位置與事件時點。單一股票不能脫離它所在的市場背景來理解。

Before any directional call is made, the system reviews market regime, sector position, peer context, and event timing. A stock should not be judged in isolation.

3. Specialist 獨立簡報 3. Independent specialist briefs

技術面、籌碼面、基本面、催化因子、風控、策略選擇與驗證角色會先各自形成初步看法。這能避免第一個強勢敘事過早主導整場會議。

Technical, chip-flow, fundamental, catalyst, risk, strategy-selection, and validation specialists each produce an initial view independently so that the first strong narrative does not dominate the whole meeting.

4. 策略提名 4. Strategy nomination

`v1.3` 會先由 Strategy Architect 判斷 setup 最適合哪一種策略模組，並標記 primary strategy、secondary strategy 與不該採用的執行方式。

In `v1.3`, the Strategy Architect first classifies the setup into the most suitable strategy family, then labels the primary strategy, secondary strategy, and the execution style to avoid.

5. 驗證面板 5. Validation panel

Quant Validation Analyst 會用績效、尾部風險、交易品質與穩健度指標檢查 setup 是否足以支撐操作。這一步的目標，是防止會議只剩下漂亮的故事。

The Quant Validation Analyst tests whether the setup deserves action by reviewing performance, tail-risk, trade quality, and robustness metrics. This step exists to prevent the meeting from becoming story-driven.

6. 交叉質詢與權重整合 6. Cross-examination and weighted synthesis

主持 agent 會在 strategy fit 與 validation 結果都進場後，才整合各 specialist 的意見。最終結論不是投票平均，而是依角色權重與風險反對意見共同形成。

The chair agent synthesizes the specialists only after strategy fit and validation are both on the table. The final verdict is not a simple vote, but a weighted conclusion shaped by both conviction and visible objections.

7. 決策文件輸出 7. Decision packet

最後輸出會包含 thesis、策略模組選擇、validation panel、base/bull/bear 三情境、建議買入區、進場條件、停損、停利階梯、失效條件，以及保留下來的 dissent memo。

The final packet includes the thesis, strategy-module selection, validation panel, base/bull/bear scenarios, preferred buy zone, entry triggers, stop-loss, take-profit ladder, invalidation, and preserved dissent notes.
